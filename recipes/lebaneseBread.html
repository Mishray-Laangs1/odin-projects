Lebanese Bread

This is our special dish of Lebanese Mountain Bread

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (or a mix of flours like whole wheat and white)
  • Water
  • Yeast (or sometimes baking powder, depending on the variation)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (optional, for softness and flavor)

Steps for preparations

  1. Mix the Dough

    2. Combine flour, water, salt, and yeast in a large bowl. Mix until a soft, slightly sticky dough forms.

  2. Knead and Rest

    3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rest for 1 – 2 hours until it doubles in size.

  3. Divide and Shape

    4. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each one into a thin circle—almost paper-thin—using a rolling pin or by hand.

  4. Preheat the Saj or Griddle

    5. Heat a domed saj (or an upside-down wok or hot griddle) until very hot. No oil is needed.

  5. Cook the Bread

    6. Place the thin dough circle on the hot surface. Cook for about 30 – 60 seconds per side until it bubbles and lightly browns. Flip once.
