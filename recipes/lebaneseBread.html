This is our special dish of Lebanese Mountain Bread

Ingredients

  • Lebanese Bread
  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Seed-tomatoes
  • Cucumbers

Steps for preparations

  1. Open the Lebanese Bread
  2. Rub the Mayonnaise
  3. Slices the Seed-tomatoes
  4. Cut the Seed-tomatoes and Cucumbers
  5. Put all together and wrap it then served
