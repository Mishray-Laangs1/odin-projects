Za'atar

This is our special dish of Za'atar Bread

Ingredients

  • Dried thyme
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Salt
  • Oregano (or sometimes marjoram, depending on the regional variation)
  • Sumac (gives it a tangy flavor)

Steps for preparations

  1. Toast the Sesame Seeds

    2. Lightly toast the sesame seeds in a dry pan over medium heat until golden and fragrant. Let them cool.

  2. Gather the Herbs

    3. Combine dried thyme, oregano (or marjoram), and any other dried herbs you prefer.

  3. Mix the Ingredients

    4. In a bowl, mix the toasted sesame seeds, dried herbs, sumac, and salt evenly.

  4. Adjust to Taste

    5. Taste the blend and add more sumac for tanginess or salt as needed.

  5. Store Properly

    6. Transfer the za'atar mix to an airtight container and keep it in a cool, dry place for up to a few months.
