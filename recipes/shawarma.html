Shawarma

This is our special dish of shawarma

Ingredients

  • Meat (commonly chicken, beef, or lamb)
  • Lemon juice (for acidity and flavor)
  • Yogurt (used in the marinade to tenderize the meat)
  • Garlic
  • Shawarma spices (a blend often including cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, and allspice)

Steps for preparations

  1. Prepare the Marinade

    2. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, and shawarma spices in a bowl to create a flavorful marinade.

  2. Marinate the Meat

    3. Slice the meat thinly and coat it well in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

  3. Cook the Meat

    4. Grill, pan-fry, or roast the marinated meat until it's fully cooked and slightly crispy on the edges.

  4. Prepare the Wrap or Plate

    5. Warm flatbread (like pita or mountain bread), and prepare toppings such as pickles, tomatoes, onions, lettuce, and garlic or tahini sauce.

  5. Assemble and Serve

    6. Place the cooked shawarma meat on the bread, add toppings and sauces, roll it up, and enjoy!
